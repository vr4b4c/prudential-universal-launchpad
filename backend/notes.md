## Objective

Generate a quiz for user to collect information about their lifestyle, financial situation and retirements plans. The responses will be used to generate shotlists for **aspirational retirement videos** that showcase a person's fulfilled future life. These are short-form (45-60 second) cinematic pieces for Prudential's "Flash Forward" campaign, illustrating how thoughtful retirement planning leads to a rich, meaningful life.
